DONATE! DONATE! DONATE!

2015 became known as “the first generation in history that [could] end extreme poverty” by the World Bank. This is because individuals, like us who donated, gave such large sources of charitable givings that caused global poverty to be cut in half, and extreme poverty in every region of developing countries to decline.

Let this 2020 year be just as memorable. If the United States is suffering this much from the covid-19 outbreak, think about and imagine how others who lack adequate sanitation are doing in third world countries.
