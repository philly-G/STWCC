Save Our Children!
Children in third world countries are counting on us, as well as many others from around the world, to support them.

Hunger is the number one cause of death.

Millions of children living in developing countries die from diarrhea due to the lack fo access to clean water and food each year. 28% of them are estimated to be underweight or stunted.

This is also a fight against poverty.

One quarter of homeless people are children.

Over 25 million children live as refugees. Due to war and oppression, over 40 million of them were not registered at birth, and most of them either die or are forced into child labor and sexual slavery unless they flee

All children are innocent.

They deserve all the help and support they recieve. It is heartbreaking that they are neglected and used.

Be apart of change, and learn how you can help them.
